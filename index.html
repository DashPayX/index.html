Protocol Overview

What is DashPayX?

DashPayX (DPX) is a peer-to-peer digital asset designed for fast payments, predictable fees, and easy staking. Inspired by battle-tested networks like Dash and Bitcoin, DPX focuses on real-world usability — not just speculation.

Built for everyday payments
Low fees • Fast confirmation
Send DPX across the globe in seconds with low-cost, on-chain transfers. The goal is simple: make crypto feel as natural as sending a message.

Earn While You Hold

DPX Staking

DPX staking is designed to be simple: stake tokens into a non-custodial smart contract, earn rewards over time, and withdraw whenever you’re ready.

How staking works

1. Connect your wallet on the staking dApp (coming soon).
2. Approve the staking contract to use your DPX.
3. Stake DPX and start earning rewards instantly.
4. Claim rewards or exit at any time.

Non-custodial Transparent on-chain logic No “lock-in” by design*

*Lock periods, if ever introduced, will be optional and clearly explained.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

A quick, honest FAQ for early community members. No buzzwords, no unrealistic promises.

Is DashPayX live on mainnet?
Not yet. You are currently viewing the prototype phase on BNB Smart Chain testnet. Mainnet deployment will only happen after contracts are reviewed and tokenomics are finalized.
Can I invest real money now?
At this stage, DashPayX is a technical and economic experiment. Nothing on this page is an invitation to invest. Never risk funds you cannot afford to lose.
Will there be governance for DPX holders?
Yes. A simple, on-chain governance module is planned so DPX holders can vote on key protocol changes, treasury decisions, and roadmap priorities.
What makes DashPayX different?
DashPayX focuses on three things: payment usability, transparent staking, and risk-aware growth. The goal is to build slowly and carefully rather than chasing quick hype.
